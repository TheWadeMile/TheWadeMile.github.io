The Wade Mile

What is the Wade Mile?

A dumb idea imagined by two friends during a run in beautiful Humboldt County, California
  • Wade a distance of 1 mile in approximately waist-deep water
  • No swimming, jumping, or "paddling" of any sort
  • Keep hands generally above water
  • No gear used to improve force generated in water
