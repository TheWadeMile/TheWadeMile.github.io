The Wade Mile

What is the Wade Mile?

  • Wade a distance of 1 mile in approximately waist-deep water.
  • No swimming. Paddling with your hands is ok.
  • No gear used to improve force generated in water.
  • Avoid current-aided routes (i.e. down a river).
